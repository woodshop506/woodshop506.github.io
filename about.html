About

I'm a seasoned full-stack developer with over 10 years of experience making web applications and sites on a variety of platforms and languages. Throughout that time, I have been a passionate advocate for leveraging current and emerging technologies to make a better experience for users and developers alike.

I've found that keeping the UI and presentation layers in the browser and serving data through an API is the most promising architecture for both today and the future. Consequently, I spend the majority of my time developing for the frontend using a variety of frameworks and libraries like Angular, React, Lo-Dash, Backbone, and Browserify (just to name a few).

Given my preference for the frontend I tend to prefer light API wrappers around an ORM/datastore for the backend, but I've worked on projects in Rails, Django (& Flask), Node/Express, and plain ol' PHP. I've even worked in VBScript prior to ASP.NET, but please don't make me do that again.

I believe in and contribute to open source software, and I've released a number of packages both for Node.js and the browser. I like package management systems like Bower and npm, and tend to use a task runner like Grunt or Gulp to automate repetetive tasks.

The internet is arguably one of the most important technologies that we've invented, and I'm thrilled to be a part of it.
